## Key Insights

1. **Southwest Growth**: Heavy investment in SW Marion County (3 new schools: high school + 2 elementary)
2. **Capacity Crisis**: Adding 7,675 new student stations across district
3. **Aging Infrastructure**: Multiple school replacement projects needed
4. **Funding Strategy**: Large COP issuance ($314M) in 2024-25 to jumpstart construction
5. **Surtax Timing**: New half-cent surtax started Jan 1, 2025 to support these projects
