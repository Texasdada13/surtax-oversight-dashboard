About the Surtax

In November 2024, Marion County voters approved a half-cent sales tax dedicated to improving school facilities. This 10-year initiative will fund critical infrastructure improvements including:

  • New Construction - Building new schools to accommodate growth
  • Renovations - Updating aging facilities
  • Safety & Security - Enhanced security systems district-wide
  • Technology - Modern learning environments
